Our Services

Financial Management

JV Property Management oversees levy billing, budgeting, reserve fund planning, and ongoing financial reporting. Financial records are maintained accurately and presented in a clear, consistent format to support trustee oversight and owner communication.

Compliance Support

JV Property Management supports trustees through ongoing governance administration, compliance oversight, and structured documentation, ensuring regulatory requirements are addressed as part of routine scheme management.

Maintenance & Operations

JV Property Management coordinates maintenance activities, contractor engagement, and operational records to support predictable upkeep, cost control, and clear accountability across the scheme.

Reporting & Communication

JV Property Management provides structured reporting and centralised access to scheme information to support informed decision-making and consistent communication across trustees, owners, and service providers.

About JVPM

JV Property Management provides structured systems and documented processes that support responsible decision-making and assist trustees in meeting their statutory obligations.
